Cookies Policy

Last Updated: October 24, 2023

1. What are Digital Seeds (Cookies)?

To make our website flourish, we use "cookies"—small data files placed on your device. Much like a plant remembers the direction of the sun, cookies help our website remember your preferences and actions over time so we can provide a smoother experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several vital functions in our digital garden:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function, such as navigating between pages.
  • Preference Cookies: These help us remember your settings, like your preferred gardening zone or language.
  • Analytical Cookies: These help us understand how visitors interact with our content, allowing us to grow and improve our resources.

3. The Types of Cookies We Plant

Type Purpose Duration
Session Cookies Temporary memory while you browse. Ends when browser closes.
Preference Cookies Remembers your botanical interests. Up to 1 year.
Analytics (Google) Helps us track site performance. Variable.

4. Managing Your Garden Settings

You have the power to "weed" out cookies at any time. Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. Please note that disabling essential cookies may prevent parts of our site from blossoming correctly for you.
