Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 24, 2023

1. Our Commitment to Your Data

At Darksoil Bloom Collective, we believe that privacy is like healthy soil—it is the foundation upon which trust grows. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you interact with our botanical resources and services.

2. Information We Cultivate

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, email address, and phone number when you fill out our contact form.
  • Gardening Preferences: Information about your soil type or botanical interests provided during inquiries.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses and browsing behavior used solely to improve our website's user experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to provide you with the best gardening expertise possible, specifically for:

  • Responding to your inquiries about soil health and plant care.
  • Sending the Collective newsletter (only if you have opted in).
  • Improving our seasonal gardening guides and digital tools.

4. Protection of Data

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. Your trust is more valuable to us than any data harvest.

5. Your Rights

Just as you have the right to prune your garden, you have the right to manage your data. You may contact us at any time to request a copy of the data we hold about you or to ask that we "uproot" (delete) your information from our systems.
